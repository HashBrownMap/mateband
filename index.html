Creating Safe-Spaces

Large-Scale Festivals

Festivals are a great way to spend time with friends, meet new people, and let your inhibitions lose!

MateBand ensures that you will stay hydrated throughout the festival and connected to security if ever you need help!

Lonely Neighbourhoods

Walking in an unknown area is nobody’s favourite past time.

If you ever come into contact with a suspicious individual who is showing any form of aggression - MateBand will ensure that you get the help you need, immediately.

Late-Night Commute

Going home alone after a long day of working? With MateBand, you are never really alone.

The band ensures constant contact with those closest to you so you can feel safe on your commute back home. Safety is at the touch of your wrist.

Amusement Parks

Losing a child amidst a large-crowd may be the scariest moment for a parent or guardian. With MateBand, you never have to worry about the safety of your child.

Receive alerts from your children if they are ever in need of help - and be instantly connected to them through the help of MateBand.

Workplace

Safety at the workplace should be a given. Unfortunately, many vocations such as mining, industrial, or over-night jobs are not risk-free.

With MateBand, a call for safety will always be heard. No phone? No problem. MateBand has got you covered.
